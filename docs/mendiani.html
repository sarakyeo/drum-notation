Chapter 2 Mendiani

2.1 Location

Guinea

2.2 Ethnic group

Malinke

2.3 Purpose

Mendiani is an initiated dance for young girls between the ages of 8 and 12 who must be virgins. After Ramadan, they are carried out to the village bara on the shoulders of men, then they dance.
